---
title: 'Create a SyncTeam'
---

1\. Navigate to "My Teams".

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/41841fd0-febf-4441-a7a0-f2a40a495970/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1376,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=99,204)


2\. Click "Create Team"

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/60ac82c1-a767-4203-995d-73c6b999decb/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1376,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=274,103)


3\. Select any integrated workspace as a source.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/7548cce3-2f53-4297-a0f1-1fdc73ea2ea3/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,0&br_px=1541,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=756,134)


4\. Fill the respective form fields and select filter if required.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/75bab8f4-41be-4602-84e8-bdb4fa0909df/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,0&br_px=1541,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=828,203)


5\. Select any integrated workspace as a replica.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/9f0fc3d7-8c15-4980-a4b8-82ad8e8248aa/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=749,400)


6\. Fill the respective form field and click "Next".

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/3dec88ad-2633-4fc4-9fa9-17dd956e5203/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=1022,346)


7\. Fill up the form and click "Submit".

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/e4ea3865-4402-4181-8290-3f29f36229f1/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,0&br_px=1541,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=859,182)


8\. A new team has been created. In our example, we selected "Google" as the source and "Slack" as a replica workspace.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/b438a17e-c968-4805-8e16-85d962bfe088/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,149&br_px=1376,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=285,415)
