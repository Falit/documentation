title: 'Generate Team Report'
---



1\. Navigate to "My Teams".

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-12/085568b0-92bd-4be3-a054-1870b5cb3616/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1376,769&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=104,204)


2\. Select "Generate Report" option from dropdown.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-12/164ec391-58f4-4a30-8878-e246982a5f07/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=557,345)


3\. Enter the email address to which the report will be shared.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-12/e0dee7ab-c0fe-4d98-8d11-e759b2eeda7e/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,110&br_px=1541,879&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=531,276)


4\. Use the calendar widgets to select the desired report date range.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-12/68582602-3e9d-47e8-8e84-b47a80edc197/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=605,517)


5\. Click "Generate Report"

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-12/cbb47c00-748f-4e4d-b124-19a76203efa6/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=602,307)


6\. Success! Report will be generated shortly.
