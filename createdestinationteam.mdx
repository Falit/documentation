1\. Select an integrated workspace as replica workspace.

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/fff27d2b-6975-4d35-901e-4fdeccbaae04/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=700,397)

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/f7f04594-eb09-4be4-a725-58071718ef35/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,102&br_px=1541,871&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=1047,276)

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/d0dc20f8-077e-43d0-9d96-be26e0173a93/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=754,436)

![](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-04-11/59a48033-d138-4324-805e-b03429f630fc/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=164,149&br_px=1541,919&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=857,416)
